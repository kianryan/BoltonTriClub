Elton Reservoir - mid May to End of August

Directions to Elton Sailing Club.
If travelling down Bolton Road (A58) towards Bury turn right at Wellington Pub into Kitchener Street, signposted Elton Reservoir and Alpha Hospital. At end of Kitchener St turn right into Buller Street. Turn left over a small bridge signposted Alpha Hospital. Once over bridge bear right and down lane to Sailing Club.

Don't rely on a sat nav, it will take you towards the reservoir from the wrong side along a lane which has a locked barrier
