JUNIORS - RUN & BIKE SESSIONS

Monday 19.00 - 20.30 (Excludes Bank Holidays)

After a warm up we usually split into 3 ability groups each with it's own coach for a half hour run session followed by a half hour cycling session on the running track. Besides improving fitness we work on improving running technique, bike handling skills and transition skills using fun activities.

The MINIMUM AGE IS 7 and children must be able to ride a bike. Everyone needs to bring a helmet, their own bike and a drink. Warm clothing (3 layers on top) and gloves are needed for cycling in winter. We do have a small number of bikes and helmets to lend out.

ADULTS - RUN & SPIN CLASS

Monday 19.00 - 20.30 (Excludes Bank Holidays)

Monday night training sessions are split into two groups. There is a group who runs on the track with sessions designed to increase speed and endurance, after this has finished the group goes indoors and either takes part in a spinning session or circuit training.

The second group takes part in a coached spinning session, followed by either a run of about four miles, or more spinning, circuit training and stretches.
