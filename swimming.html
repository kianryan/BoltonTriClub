Club Swimming Sessions

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Wednesday 7-8.15pm

Ladybridge Leisure Centre
New York, Junction Rd, Deane, Bolton, BL3 4NG
TEL: +44 01204 334432

Google+ map...

Adults £3

This session is for competent front crawl swimmers who want to work on speed, stamina and technique.
Not suitable for novices. Under 16s by invitation (see your coach)

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Saturday 3-5.30pm

Farnworth Leisure Centre
136 Brackley St, Farnworth, BL4 9DZ
TEL: +44 1204 334477

Google+ map...

Juniors (Saturday 3 - 4pm) £3

We have 3 ability groups. The session is in lanes in a 25m pool and so it is not suitable for non-swimmers. Our coaches can improve swim technique and fitness and swimmers can progress through the 3 groups.

New starters need to come for a short trial first to ensure they are safe to swim lengths and to determine which group they should go into. (MINIMUM AGE IS 7)

*Swimmers need goggles, a drink and after a few sessions they will need to get their own pair of fins.

Adults (Saturday 4 - 5.30pm) £4

This session is for all levels of adults from front crawl beginners to advanced swimmers but not for non-swimmers. There are 3 ability groups and swimmers can progress through the lanes.

*Swimmers need goggles, a drink and after a few sessions they will need to get their own pair of fins.

The Saturday and Wednesday swim sessions above are included in the Bolton Tri Club standing order scheme. So if you are a BTC member who pays for sessions by standing order you won't have to pay the session fee at these sessions.

Open Swim Sessions

organised jointly by Bolton Tri Club and Bolton Metro Swim Squad (BMSS)

These sessions cater for all levels of swimmer aged 16+ from novice to elite and are coached by professional coaches from BMSS who can provide expert analysis and feedback on your stroke. You don't need to join Bolton Tri Club but after 3 sessions all swimmers must join the Amateur Swimming Association which costs £14

Open Water Swimming with Uswim

Wednesdays 5.30 to 8pm, Saturdays 8 to 11am

Dock 9 Salford Quays

Session fee £4, Uswim Bronze membership £10

We are unable to use Elton Reservoir due to maintenance work in 2016 so far so we have become a tri club partner with Uswim. Go to uswim open water memberships and take out a bronze membership and take your registration e-mail and BTC membership card with you to get the reduced £4 session fee.

IMPORTANT INFO:

• To attend swim sessions you will need to fill out a coaching consent/medical form.
(After 2 sessions we will ask you to join the club)

• We will also ask you to join the club for open water sessions. This is for insurance and safety purposes.
