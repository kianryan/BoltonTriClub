ONE FOR THE DIARY...

  • August 7th

    36th Annual British Transplant Games will commence in Bolton.

    The primary aim for the Games is to raise awareness for the importance of Organ Donation and to celebrate the gift of life and physical wellbeing..

  • August16th

    Electric Swim Saturday (Elton Reservoir Bury)

    Starting after dark swim 400m, 800m or 1 mile with special buoys and UV lights to mark the course. Food and entertainment. Visit www.train2swim.com/events-2 to find out more, food and entertainment. £10 from every entry goes to Alder Hey and Kidscan UK charities

  • August17th

    Salford Triathlon

    A fast flat course round the Media City complex at Salford Quays and great for spectators. Salford Standard Distance Triathlon: 1.5km Swim | 40km Bike | 10km Run

  • Sept21st

    North West Junior Club Championships Blackpool

    A normal junior triathlon that anyone can enter, the difference being that club members will not only score points towards the North West Junior Series, they will also be scoring points for their club towards the North West Junior Club Championship. This event was a fantastic success last year with over 250 entrants.

  • August31st

    Salt Ayre Junior Duathlon 12 noon to 4pm

    Best venue in the NW for junior triathlon. 8 lane pool, 1.3k tarmac off road cycle track and running track all on same site. Salt Ayre Junior Duathlon

YOUR CLUB NEEDS YOU...

Bolton Tri Club is run entirely by volunteers and without them the club would cease to exist.

We would like every member to contribute in some way to the running of the club; many hands make light work and a better club.
