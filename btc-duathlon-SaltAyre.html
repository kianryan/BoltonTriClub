BTC/SALT AYRE JUNIOR DUATHLON AND ADULT G0-TRI DUATHLON

Sunday 5th April 2020- (13:00-16:00)

Salt Ayre Sports Centre, Doris Henderson Way, Lancaster, LA1 5JS

Google+ map...

Races for all ages and abilities. The Tristar and Youth races are part of the Triathlon England NW Series. The short distance children’s duathlon and adult go-tri duathlon - ideal events for newcomers.

Electronic chip timing
Music and Commentary
Goody bags and race medals for all participants
Run on athletics track. Youth and Go-Tri events also run on cycle paths
Junior events in age group order starting with Tristart at 1pm
Go Tri Duathlon for adults approx start 3.15pm

The ages for each event are in brackets and are the ages on 31.12.20.
Enter on-line only. Entries close 31/03/2020 - 11am
