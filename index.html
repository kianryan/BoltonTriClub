Welcome to Bolton Triathlon Club

We are a growing, friendly, fun club which aims to help all our members enjoy
swimming, cycling and running.

Beginners, young and old, are catered for alongside more experienced triathletes of all standards. Come along our Saturday swim session at Farnworth pool or our Monday night run/bike session at Leverhulme Park track or our Wednesday track session at Leverhulme Park and we will introduce you to a coach and the most appropriate training group.

TRAINING AND COACHING:

Sessions every day of the week throughout the year with guidance and encouragement from our qualified coaches. Training with others is a good way to get motivated, learn about the sport and make new friends and training partners. If you need to improve or learn front crawl, get used to open-water swimming or find out how to plan your training we can help.

RACING:

Our members participate and compete at all distances from Sprint to Ironman. Our juniors take part in children's and youth events that are part of the Triathlon England NW series. Besides multi-sport events (triathlons, duathlons and aquathons) many of us take part in open-water swimming, running, cross-country and cycling events.
Member that race regulary are encouraged to join Triathlon England

TALENT DISCOVERY & ACHIEVING YOUR POTENTIAL:

You or your child may not even know that you are good at triathlon. Our coaches will be able to spot your abilities and help you develop. We have members who have gone on to win age group events or qualify for GB age group teams and juniors who have gone on to represent the NW region at Inter-Regional championships. But whatever your potential we can help you achieve it.

NO PRESSURE:

Many of our members don't compete in races and that's fine by us. They just come to keep fit or do some exercise whilst their children are also training. After a while though you might catch the bug and the confidence and want to do an event.

JUNIOR SECTION:

We have a big junior section ages 7 to 16 and use a variety of activities to improve skills and fitness. We have bikes to lend at Monday night sessions if you can't bring your own. We have been awarded the Clubmark accreditation by Sport England which ensures a high standard of welfare for children and young people.

A CLUB FOR ALL:

Apart from being able to swim 100m and ride a bike, there is no qualifying standard; we welcome all abilities, shapes and sizes. We welcome all sections of the community regardless of gender, age, sexuality, nationality, religion or ethnic origin. And if you are a disabled person we will do our best to include you.
