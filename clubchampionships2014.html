BTC Championship Events 2014

The following events will be club championship events where the first male and female Bolton Tri Club members to finish will win club champion awards. These are presented at our Annual Presentation Night and Party and winners will go down in history on our Roll of Honour. In the Aquathlon there will also be an under 17s award.

Duathlon (Sunday 14th April)

Come and support Decathlon Bolton's First Event since opening in 2011. The Run is a mixture of Trail and Road and the Cycle is an out and back route. Entry packs are available from the store..
Decathlon Bolton Duathlon: 6k Run | 28k Bike | 6k Run

---------------------------

Sprint Distance Triathlon (Sunday 13th July)

A well established event, great for first timers in lovely Cheshire countryside. One of the warmest open water swims on the circuit. With the first wave at 10am there is even a civilised start time!
Sprint Distance Triathlon: 750m Swim | 14 Miles Bike | 3 Miles Run

---------------------------

Standard Distance Triathlon (17th August)

A fast flat course round the Media City complex at Salford Quays and great for spectators.
Salford Standard Distance Triathlon: 1.5km Swim | 40km Bike | 10km Run

---------------------------

Aquathlon: Early September (TBC)

This is our own fun event for club members and a good way to finish the season.
Elton Reservoir: 1km Swim | 40km Bike | 10km Run

---------------------------

Women Only Triathlon (27th July)

Organised by Chester Tri Club this is a well-known, popular event which won the British Triathlon, "Event of the Year" award in 2009. Many people see triathlon as a sport for those who are super fit but Chester Tri Club are encouraging more and more ladies to start training and take part in achievable, short distance races
Deva Divas Triathlon: 750m Swim | 25km Bike | 5 Mile Run

YOUR CLUB NEEDS YOU...

We would like every member to contribute in some way to the running of the club; many hands make light work and a better club.
