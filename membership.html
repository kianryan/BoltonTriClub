BTC Memberships

The club offers a range of membership packages to suit different family situations and commitment levels. To attend sessions you will need to fill out a medical/consent form and bring it to your first session. After two sessions adults will need to join and after one session juniors will need to join the club. This is for insurance and safety purposes.

12 month membership subscriptions are charged as follows;

Junior - (17 or less at 31st January 2020. Min age 7) - £15

Student - (full time education) - £15

Senior - (18 or over at 1st Feburary 2020) - £35

Family membership (Max 2 adults + Max 4 children - living at the same address) - £55

Fees are non-refundable and cover the period 1st February 2020 to 31st January 2021.

Medical/Consent form

The medical/consent form will be needed to be brought along to your first session.
