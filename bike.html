Bike Spin Class (Monday)

Leverhulme Park Community Leisure Centre
Long Lane, Bolton, BL2 6EB
TEL: +44 1204 334272

Google+ map...

ADULTS £3

Monday (Excludes Bank Holidays)

19.00 - 19.45 and 19.45 - 20.30

Indoor cycling class requires no co-ordination as it's a freestyle stationary bike workout. That means you get to control the intensity as the instructor leads you across steep hills, fast highways and other terrain. Throughout it all, a selection of popular music will keep you motivated but don't forget your towel – pedal power can get rather hot!.

Benefits include: fantastic fat burner, tones your whole body, increases endurance.

Cycling Time Trials

Bolton Triathlon Clubs is affiliated to Cycling Time Trials (CTT)

Being affiliated to Cycling Time Trials allows Club members to take part in all Time Trials organised under the auspices of the governing body. If we wish, we can also hold time trial events as a result of this affiliation. This is very good news for those interested in taking part in time trials and we would recommend members take part in these events. Cycling Time Trials
