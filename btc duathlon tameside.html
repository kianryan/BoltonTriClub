Bolton Tri Club


2019 Tameside Junior Duathlon
and Adult Go-Tri Duathlon -

Early bird price up to 9th July

Sunday 11th August 2019 - 12-5pm

Tameside cycle circuit, Roy Oldam sports village, Richmond st, Ashton-under-Lyne, OL7 9HG

Google+ map...

Races for all ages and abilities. The Tristar and Youth races are part of the Triathlon England NW Series. The short distance children’s duathlon and adult go-tri duathlon - ideal events for newcomers.

Electronic chip timing
Music and Commentary
Goody bags and race memento for all participants
Run on grass playing field
Cycle on tarmac off-road 1km cycle circuit

The ages for each event are in brackets and are the ages on 31.12.19.
Enter on-line only. Entries close 06/08/2019 - 23.30pm
